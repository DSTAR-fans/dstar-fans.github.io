**D-STAR Quick – Video 1: First Time Call Sign Setup (Icom ID-51)**

[INTRO – 12 seconds]
"Welcome to D-STAR Quick — short bites to get you on the air fast.  
I’m Mark, KE8BZW.  
Today’s goal: Set your callsign on a brand-new or blank ID-51 so every digital transmission correctly identifies you and your radio model — exactly what the FCC wants to see."

[MAIN BODY – ~80 seconds]
"Start by powering on your radio.

Press the MENU button.

Use the D-pad to highlight SET, then press the center button to enter.

Scroll down to page 2 of the SET menu and select My Station.

Now choose My Call Sign.

Press the QUICK button on the bottom right, then select Edit.

Twist the control dial to set each letter of your callsign — K-E-8-B-Z-W — and after each character push the right D-pad to move to the next spot.

When you’ve finished your callsign, keep pressing the right D-pad until the cursor moves over to the slash area.

Now enter your radio model — put /51. This short standard helps everyone in the logs instantly know what radio you’re using.

Press ENTER to save.

Back out of the menu with the left D-pad.

To double-check, go back into My Call Sign and you’ll see your full callsign and the slash in the MY field at the bottom of the screen."
