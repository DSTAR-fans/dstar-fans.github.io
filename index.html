Simplex Frequencies

It's easy to make use of D-STAR without any repeaters or hotspots. Start by setting your radio for 145.6700 (the National 2m D-STAR Calling Frequency) and put your radio into DV mode. Just key the mic and you're operating with D-STAR!

Here are a few other frequencies to try:

2m70cm
145.6000 DV441.0000 DV
145.6700 DV (Calling)445.6700 DV

D-STAR simplex operation allows you to:

  1. Send your callsign with every transmission (though it's still advisable to ID via digital voice)
  2. Send your location if GPS is enabled, or the GPS coordinates are programmed into your base station (great for pointing directional antennas)
  3. Sending pictures via mobile devices or transferred to a supporting radio via memory card (you can even talk while you transmit)
  4. Send short text messages along with each transmission, as well as host text chats using the RS-MS1A or RS-MS1I apps.
